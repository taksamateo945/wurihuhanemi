    Go Behind The Scenes On Allegras Music Video All About Us

    Opening up onto a rustic urban setting, the camera swoops in on all the fun-filled action Allegras music video All About Us explores. A visual that depicts the youthfulness of this rising star, Allegra immerses herself in her pastel-coloured world.

    06 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Opening up onto a rustic urban setting, the camera swoops in on all the fun-filled action Allegra’s music video ‘All About Us’ explores. A visual that depicts the youthfulness of this rising star, Allegra immerses herself in her pastel-coloured world. 

    ‘All About Us’ is the breakout single from rising star Allegra. Having already achieved immense success as a standalone single, with the track reaching respectable positions in Music Week’s pop/dance charts and Billboards dance chart too, the accompanying bold and dynamic music video highlights the modernity of this young artist. The derelict aesthetic value combined with the trend-setting style give the visual a genuine youthful glow, showcasing exactly who Allegra is as an artist. 

    Going beyond the surface of the fun-filled music video for Allegra’s breakout anthem, the ‘All About Us’ behind the scenes look sees cameras galore chasing around empty areas of East London, following Allegra and co cruising around on bikes, skates and swegways on their mission to live life to the fullest extent. 

    For Allegra, the behind the scenes release of ‘All About Us’ is the next step in the beginning of a blossoming music career, which is set to see more material being released by this up and coming talent soon.
