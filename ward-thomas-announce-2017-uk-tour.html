    Fresh from wrapping-up their sold out UK and Ireland Cartwheels Tour, Ward Thomas have announced a brand new tour for 2017.

    The 22 date UK tour kicks off in Guildford on April 28th, 2017, and ends in Cambridge on May 30th. Ireland dates are set to be announced at a later date!

    The news of their huge 2017 tour comes after a hugely successful 2016 saw them became the first ever British country music act to top the UK album charts with their sublime ‘Cartwheels’ LP, embark on a sold out tour, have their song ‘Guilty Flowers’ played on Radio 1, perform at the Royal Albert Hall at the Children in Need Rocks for Terry concert, etc, etc….

    The full UK tour dates are as follows;

    Tickets for Ward Thomas’ 2017 tour are available via pre-sale tomorrow from 9am, and on general sale from Friday November 11th.

    You will be able to catch Ward Thomas performing Dolly Parton’s huge worldwide hit ‘9 to 5’ during the TV broadcast of Children in Need Rocks for Terry on November 14th on BBC 1.
