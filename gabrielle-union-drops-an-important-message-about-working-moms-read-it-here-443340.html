    Gabrielle Union shared an important message about working moms on her social media account. You can also check it out below.

    ‘Did you know one in three working moms have considered leaving the workforce or downshifting their career during the pandemic? Womxn, especially Black women, are shouldering the worst of 2020, so I’m working with @SecretDeodorant to #RaiseItUp for working moms and women everywhere! ‘ Gabrielle wrote.

    She continued said: ‘Now, I want you to #RaiseItUp too. Post an image of yourself and how you #RaiseItUp and tell me who you #RaiseItUp for- tag @secretdeodorant and use #RaiseItUp. Let’s raise our voices and each other! #SecretDeoPartner #AllStrengthNoSweat.’

    Someone else said: ‘raise it, girl! I raise my hand with you… I love you,’ and a commenter posted this: ‘You said a lot right here. I definitely am considering my options cause I am burnt out with working FT and homeschooling FT.’

    A fan posted: ‘I left my job because the entire building refused to wear masks. A FEDERAL JOB 🤦🏽‍♀️’ and someone else said: ‘I had no idea women were feeling the way I do. I’m so overwhelmed with my daily life that my career seems unmanageable at its current structure. Thank you for this.’

    One follower posted this message: ‘Please Discuss the atrocities against Armenia by Turkey and Azerbaijan who are trying to finish the genocide they attempted 105 years ago. This is human rights issues and demands the attention and respect from the media and those in power.’

    Someone else said: ‘Love this. Thank you for using your platform and voice to help all of us shine! You are magical!’

    Another Instagrammer posted: ‘I’m just looking forward to the day were saying, “you gotta laugh, to keep, from crying” becomes a thing of the past! A sista is tired…😩’
