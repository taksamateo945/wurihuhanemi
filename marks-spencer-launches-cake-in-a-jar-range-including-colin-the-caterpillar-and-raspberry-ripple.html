    Marks & Spencer have delighted fans by launching cake jars today for just £4.

    Perfectly portioned for packing in a picnic under the relaxed lockdown rules, as well for lunch on the go, fans have already shared their delight at the sweet treat.    

    Available in three delicious flavours at all UK stores, the range includes a fruity raspberry ripple, indulgent trillionaire’s and chocolatey Colin the Caterpillar.

    What’s in M&S Cake Jar range? 

    Colin the Caterpillar Cake Jar – £4, 178g Layers of all the Colin goodness – rich chocolate sponge, Belgian chocolate sauce, chocolate ganache, milk and white chocolate chips and finished with a Colin the Caterpillar white chocolate face.

    Raspberry Ripple Cake Jar – £4, 158g Layers of madeira sponge, sweet raspberry jam, creamy vanilla buttercream and freeze-dried raspberry pieces.

    Trillionaire’s Cake Jar – £4, 169g Layers of madeira sponge with salted caramel sauce, chocolate ganache, milk chocolate shortcake balls and fudge pieces.
