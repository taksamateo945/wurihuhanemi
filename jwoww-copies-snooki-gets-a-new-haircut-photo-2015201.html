    BFF makeovers!  

    Jenni "JWoww" Farley admitted to being inspired by her BFF Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi when she debuted a new haircut via Twitter on Jan. 15.

    The MTV reality star, 28, added shape to her dark mane with dozens of choppy layers, which created a teased, bouffant hairstyle. According to her blog, the new look was created by Joei D Fox at Sniptease Hair Studio in Toms River, N.J. 

    "Loved @Snooki's new transformation! Decided to get a haircut too… What do you think?" the new mom tweeted. 

    The day before Farley's makeover on Jan. 14, Polizzi, 27, got rid of her waist-length extensions in favor of a modern bob. "Say goodbye to this hair cause mommys getting a makeover," she captioned a picture on Instagram, which showed off the new look.
