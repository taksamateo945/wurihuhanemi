    Along with her recent relationship drama, “Love and Hip Hop” star Erica Mena found herself in the middle of another melee.

    The television personality made some startling accusations against Shad Moss aka rapper Bow Wow this week, claiming that he physically abused R&B singer Ciara. Her claims follow after the “So So Def” rapper’s ex-girlfriend and “Growing Up Hip Hop: Atlanta” co-star Kiyomi Leslie alleged she lost a baby due to one of their domestic dispute.

    In December, Leslie initially revealed that she had been pregnant and suffered a miscarriage due to the stresses of social media. Well, following a recent episode of “GUHHATL” which discussed her and the child star’s recent domestic dispute, she apparently claimed Bow Wow punched her in the stomach while she was pregnant with their child, caused her to miscarry.

    “If y’all believe that lame a– nigga didn’t do nothing. Y’all need to be in a mental home with him and his momma,” she said in now deleted tweets. “I didn’t put my bruises on tv because I don’t have too.

    Mena co-signed what the 28-year-old model said, urging fans to believe Leslie’s allegations. She then not only indicated that she too suffered at the hands of Bow Wow, but that his ex-girlfriend Ciara, whom he split with around 2006, was also battered.

    “I’m the tough girl so my story was never the truth but y’all see how round 4 played out,” Mena wrote. “He broke Ciara finger years ago, so this been going on.”

    The 31-year-old model and the Ohio native called off their engagement in 2015. Bow claimed he wasn’t ready for commitment at the time, while Mena claimed she became fed up with his abuse.

    As for Ciara, the singer has yet to confirm or deny Mena’s claims.
